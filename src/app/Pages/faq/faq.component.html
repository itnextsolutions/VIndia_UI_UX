How do I take advantage of bulk discounting?
To deliver the most competitive, every day low prices to our customers, we now offer reduced prices on eligible sale items automatically, regardless of cart size.
How do I take advantage of bulk discounting?
To deliver the most competitive, every day low prices to our customers, we now offer reduced prices on eligible sale items automatically, regardless of cart size. Sale prices are displayed directly on the product pages and notated in blue. Buy more, save more is still our motto! Eligible bulk orders $80 and up are still automatically discounted in cart. Unlike before, sale items may actually be eligible for additional bulk discounts, but at a maximum rate that factors in any reduced pricing that has already been applied.
